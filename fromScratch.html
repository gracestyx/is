The Happiest Place On Earth

Why I Love Disney World

Walt Disney World in Orlando, Florida is more than a theme park. It's a place where imagination becomes reality. Whether it's the thrill of Space Mountain, the wonder of EPCOT's World Showcase, or the nostalgia of Main Street U.S.A., there is something magical around every corner. I've been visiting since I was a kid and the excitement never fades.

The Four Parks

Disney World is made up of four amazing theme parks, each with its own unique lands and attractions:

  1. Magic Kingdom
    • Space Mountain
    • Haunted Mansion
    • Pirates of the Caribbean
    • Seven Dwarfs Mine Train
  2. EPCOT
    • Guardians of the Galaxy: Cosmic Rewind
    • Remy's Ratatouille Adventure
    • Test Track
    • World Showcase
  3. Hollywood Studios
    • Star Wars: Rise of the Resistance
    • Tower of Terror
    • Slinky Dog Dash
    • Millennium Falcon: Smugglers Run
  4. Animal Kingdom
    • Avatar Flight of Passage
    • Kilimanjaro Safaris
    • Expedition Everest
    • Na'vi River Journey
